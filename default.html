Students:

All assignments, communications, and resources will be shared with you via Google Classroom. You should login there with your Riverdale ID and password. If you have troubles with that, you should seek help on the Riverdale Web Page here.

Your official grades are kept in Synergy, which I will update on a regular basis. You should check this routinely to make sure the record matches your understanding, and that your status in the course is how you want it to be.

Parents:

Communication is important. The two most immediate ways to get information about what's going on in your child's classes are through Synergy and Google Classroom. You should have already received your Parent Vue login information from the school. If you need any help with it, please go here to get information about how to activate your account or how to get help if you're having troubles.

Google Classroom is how I share all announcements and assignments with students. They can log in, see what is going on, submit assignments, and see how they did. They can even resubmit assignments as needed. Since everything we do in most of my classes is electronic, this is an incredibly valuable tool: it ensures that the work students submit actually makes it to me. If you want, you can have your email added to your child's account and you will get updates automatically. Contact me if you would like this access. For more complete information: have them login and show you around their view in Google Classroom.
